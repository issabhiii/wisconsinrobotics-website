University Rover Challenge (URC)

Mars Rovers

Held annually in southern Utah, URC challenges student teams to design and build the next generation of Mars rovers that will one day work alongside astronauts exploring the Red Planet. We've participated since 2016, competing among hundreds of top university teams worldwide.
