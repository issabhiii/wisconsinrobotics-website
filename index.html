About Wisconsin Robotics

We are a diverse group of students passionate about robotics, working together to design, build, and compete with cutting-edge robotic systems.

Our Mission

To provide hands-on experience in robotics engineering, foster innovation, and represent UW Madison in national and international competitions.

Our Community

A collaborative environment where students from all disciplines come together to learn, build, and push the boundaries of robotics technology.

Our Achievements

Multiple competition wins, innovative projects, and successful alumni working at leading tech companies and research institutions.
